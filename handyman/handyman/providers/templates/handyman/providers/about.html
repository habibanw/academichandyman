About Us

Welcome to academicHandyMan360, a handyman service company run by Virginia Tech students, for Virginia Tech students and faculty. Our mission is to provide reliable and efficient handyman services to the Virginia Tech community, helping you with a wide range of repairs and maintenance needs.

Our Commitment

We are deeply committed to serving the Virginia Tech community. As students and members of this vibrant community ourselves, we understand the unique needs and challenges you face. Our team of skilled handymen is dedicated to delivering high-quality services with a personal touch.

Why Choose academicHandyMan360?

  • Community-Driven: We are proud to be a part of the Virginia Tech community, and our services are tailored to meet the needs of our fellow students, faculty, and staff.
  • Experienced Team: Our handymen are experienced professionals who take pride in their work, ensuring your satisfaction with every job.
  • Convenience: We understand your busy schedule, which is why we offer flexible appointment options to suit your needs.
